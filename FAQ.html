Some people win but most people lose playing that game and most of people making these claims can't be trusted. My personal belief is that the real opportunities will be found in projects harnessing blockchain tehnology to improve a vast number of systems, not in some memecoin ponzi scheme. Q: Is any of your content paid for by sponsors?

No. For the integrity of the content I do not request any money to be featured or talked about. However, I will be giving anyone and everyone the ability to pay for advertising of videos and any guests can specify their video to be promoted. In that case I would do an 85/15 split, in which 85% of goes directly to advertising paying for twitter, youtube, google or tiktok video promotion services. I am taking the 15% as a way to generate some revenue for the work, and to improve the project overall. So in a way you could say that maybe I am getting paid, but I am honestly not expecting it. There is no expectation of payment from guests, I seek projects that I find interesting and that are doing something that I think can resonate with a general audience. I am specifically planning for a no budget operation to make sure I can maintain objectivity as much as possible. I will be sure to mention any vested interest I have in any projects I talk about. My advice to everyoen is not to listen to anyone that won't tell you if they are being paid to say the things they are saying.
